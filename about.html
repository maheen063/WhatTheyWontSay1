Meet the Founder!!

Hi! My name is Inaya, and I'm thrilled to introduce myself as the founder of What They Won't Say. I'm currently a high school student with a love for meaningful conversations and what goes unsaid. You can usually find me lost in a good book or falling into deep research rabbit holes about everything from global issues to internet culture. I believe What They Won't Say can be a space where we combine serious conversations with a dose of wit, making complex issues approachable without losing their impact. Feel free to reach out if you have ideas, stories, or just want to share your thoughts!
